## 💡 Best practices for better results

To get the most out of your agents, keep these tips in mind:

*   Use specific language. Instead of saying "write something," say "write a summary of this report in four sentences."
*   Break big goals into smaller steps. Agents perform better if you give them one clear task at a time.
*   Review agent logs. The logs show you where the agent might get stuck or confused. Update your instructions based on these logs.
*   Limit the number of agents running at once. Running many agents simultaneously uses more memory and may slow your computer.
